CHEMISTRY

Chemical elements must be written in their short name

Hydrogen -> H Gold -> Au

And chemical formulas must be written in canonical form

water -> H2O sulfuric acid -> H2SO4

Reactions must be also written in canonical form

2K + 2H20 -> 2KOH + H2
